Hi Nanna is a heartwarming Telugu romantic drama film released in 2023, directed by Shouryuv. The movie stars Nani and Mrunal Thakur in lead roles, along with child artist Kiara Khanna.

The story revolves around Viraj, a loving single father, and his six-year-old daughter Mahi, who suffers from a serious health condition. Despite their challenges, they share a beautiful and emotional bond.

As Viraj begins narrating the past, a woman named Yashna enters their lives, unknowingly connected to their history. This leads to a deeply emotional journey filled with love, memories, sacrifice, and unexpected twists.

The film explores themes of:
❤️ Father–daughter love
💔 Lost memories and relationships
💫 Destiny and second chances
👨‍👧 Emotional family bonding

With soulful music by Hesham Abdul Wahab and strong performances, Hi Nanna became a superhit.
